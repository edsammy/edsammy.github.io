power quality monitor

summer 2013

This is a WiFi enabled three-phase power quality monitor. The device uses an Arudino MEGA as its brains, a MAXQ3183 power meter IC to read power quality data, and a WiFly Shield for communication. Two devices are currently installed at the University of Rochester and collecting data which can be viewed here.

This project was funded by the Xerox Corporation and the STEP of the US Department of Energy. Preliminary work was done by David Fung and Jak Yung under Professor Thomas Jones.
